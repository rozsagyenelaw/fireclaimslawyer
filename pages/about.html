Why I Fight for Fire Victims

After witnessing how insurance companies treated families following California's devastating wildfires, I made it my mission to level the playing field. Too many people accept pennies on the dollar simply because they don't know their rights or can't fight billion-dollar corporations alone.

From my office in Glendale, I've helped hundreds of fire victims across Los Angeles County recover what they deserve. Every case strengthens my resolve - these companies must be held accountable.

"Your Insurance Company Has an Army of Lawyers. Shouldn't You Have One Too?"

This is what I tell every fire victim who calls. The insurance industry counts on you being overwhelmed, underinformed, and unable to fight back. I change that equation.
